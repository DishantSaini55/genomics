About Us

Welcome to Genomics Research & Diagnostics Centre

At Genomics Research & Diagnostics Centre, we are committed to providing accurate, reliable, and timely diagnostic services to our patients. Located in the heart of Karnal, Haryana, our state-of-the-art laboratory combines cutting-edge technology with expert medical professionals to deliver superior healthcare solutions.

With years of experience in medical diagnostics and pathology, we understand the critical role that accurate test results play in diagnosis and treatment. Our laboratory is equipped with the latest diagnostic instruments and follows stringent quality control measures to ensure precision in every test we conduct.
